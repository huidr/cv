About Me

I'm a mathematics major and physics minor at IISER Mohali with research interests in differential geometry and general relativity. I like other areas of mathematics which are related to geometry and topology as well. I like to write code or pseudocode to solve or compute mathematical/scientific problems. I'm comfortable with Python and C++.

I'm something of a geek myself. I like to write CLI programs for bash (because bash is my favorite shell and why not?). I try out different editors from time to time but I alwalys return to Emacs. Sometimes I live-TeX my lecture notes using a customized AUCTeX on my Emacs. BTW I don't use Arch (some Debian-based system usually gets the job done).

Apart from academics, I occasionally find time to indulge in literature, photography and cricket. I regularly re-read Dostoevsky's The Brothers Karamazov (because I adore it and I consider it to be the greatest book I've ever read). In the past, I was interested in birdwatching and DIY projects (I once made a telescope).
